About Us

1. Introduction

Welcome to the BayaniAct – a modern digital platform designed to simplify and enhance the way barangay services are delivered to residents. Whether you're applying for a clearance, requesting a certificate, or accessing other community services, BMS is here to make the process smoother, more efficient, and more accessible.

2. Our Mission

Our mission is to empower residents by providing a user-friendly, efficient, and transparent platform for accessing essential services. We aim to modernize the way barangays operate, making it easier for residents to connect with local government officials and access services online.

3. Our Vision

Our vision is to create a smart, connected, and sustainable barangay where residents can easily interact with local authorities and access services at their convenience. We envision a future where technology serves as a bridge between citizens and the government, ensuring a higher quality of life for everyone.

4. Core Values
  • Integrity: We believe in honesty and transparency in all our interactions.
  • Efficiency: We aim to provide fast, reliable, and effective services to our residents.
  • Customer-Centric: Our platform is designed with the needs of our residents in mind.
  • Innovation: We are committed to continuously improving and innovating our services.
5. What We Do

BayaniAct provides a variety of online services to facilitate the efficient processing of requests. These include:

  • Barangay Clearance: Apply for a clearance for employment, business, or legal purposes.
  • Certificates: Request certificates of residency, indigency, or other official documents.
  • Business Permits: Apply for business-related permits and clearances.
  • Community Services: Schedule services like community events, programs, or volunteer activities.
  • Complaint Reporting: Report issues or concerns directly to barangay officials.

Through this platform, residents can access and track the status of their requests anytime, anywhere.

6. How It Works

BayaniAct allows users to register, create requests, and track their application status in real-time. The process is simple:

  1. Register/Login: Create an account or log into the system.
  2. Submit Request: Select the type of service or certificate you need and provide the required details.
  3. Payment (If Applicable): Complete any required payments through the platform.
  4. Track Status: Check the status of your request and receive notifications when it’s ready.
  5. Receive Service: Pick up or receive the requested service or certificate.
7. Our Team

BayaniAct is supported by the dedicated team of barangay officials, IT professionals, and community leaders who work together to ensure that all services are delivered efficiently. Our team is committed to continuous improvement and providing the best possible experience for the residents.
