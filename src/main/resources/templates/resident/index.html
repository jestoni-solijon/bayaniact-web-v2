Vision

A Vision to make Barangay Dioquino Zobel the cleanest, healthiest, most progressive and crime-free community with people working in synergy in building the edifice of peace, unity, progress and development towards a society under the Glory of God Almighty.

Mission

A Mission to fulfill its vision for the general welfare of its residents and to attain fullest development as self-reliant community through good and effective governance and excellent public service.

Commitment For Excellence

The Barangay Administration of Barangay Dioquino Zobel is strongly committed to the promotion of the General Welfare of its constituents by upholding truth, justice and democracy through good governance and excellence in public service in consonant with the Constitution and other laws of the Republic of the Philippines
